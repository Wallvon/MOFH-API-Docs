## Account Suspension

<Callout type="info">
**What are the comments that you could receive when an account gets suspended?**

_Permanent suspension reasons:_

- `PHISHING`
- `VIRUS_MALWARE_HOSTING`
- `NULLED_SCRIPT`
- `CONTENT_VIOLATION`
- `ABUSE_COMPLAINT`
- `SPAM_DOMAIN_SIGNUP`
- `REQUESTED`
- `ADMIN_SUSPEND`
- `ADMIN_CLOSE : <reason>`
- `RES_CLOSE : <reason>`
- `OTHER`

_Temporary suspension reasons:_

- `DAILY_IO`
- `DAILY_HIT`
- `MYSQL_OVERLOAD`

_Inactivity suspension reasons:_

- `AUTO_IDLE`

</Callout>

## Account Deletion

<Callout type="info">
**When does an account get auto-deleted?**<br />
